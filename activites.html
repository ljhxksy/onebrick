One Brick currently operates six chapters nationwide: Boston, Chicago, San Francisco, Seattle, Silicon Valley, and Builds our volunteer trip chapter.

The One Brick model has been hugely successful! Since we started, One Brick volunteers have contributed 722,000 hours to the communities we serve.

What makes us special is that One Brick chapters are 100% volunteer-run! We have no chapter based employees... we don't even maintain offices!

But we DO have a lot of dedicated volunteers who make it all happen. And we're always looking for more people like YOU to join us!
